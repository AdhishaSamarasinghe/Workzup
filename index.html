What is Workzup?

Workzup is a one-day hiring platform built for urgent, short-term work. We help employers post quick jobs and find trusted workers nearby. Job seekers discover convenient day gigs, accept bookings, and get paid fast.

Problem we solve

Employers often need immediate help for events, shifts, or temporary tasks. Traditional hiring is too slow. Workzup streamlines posting, booking, and communication for same-day staffing.

Features

Job Posting

Post jobs in under a minute with role details, location, pay, and required skills.

Job Search

Search by date, location, category, and see instant matches near you.

Booking System

Simple booking flow — employers confirm, workers accept, shift is scheduled.

Messaging

Built-in chat for clarifying shift details and coordinating logistics.

Ratings

Ratings and reviews build trust so top workers rise to the top.

How It Works

For Job Seekers

  1. Sign up and create a profile with skills and availability.
  2. Browse same-day opportunities nearby.
  3. Accept bookings and message the employer.
  4. Complete the shift and get rated.

For Employers

  1. Create a quick job with pay, time, and location.
  2. Receive applications / invite workers to book.
  3. Confirm a worker and coordinate details via chat.
  4. Complete the job and leave feedback.
