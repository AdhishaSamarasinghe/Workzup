How It Works

Workzup connects students and job seekers with verified employers for safe, transparent and flexible hourly or one-day jobs.

For Job Seekers

  1. Search jobs - Sign up, create a profile with your skills and availability, and browse same-day or upcoming shifts nerby.
  2. Get hired - Apply for suitable jobs or accept invitations from verified employers. Use in- app messaging to confirm shift details.
  3. Work and get paid - Attend the shift, complete the work and get marked as attended. Your ratings grow with every complted job, unlocking more trusted opportunities.

For Employers

  1. Post job - Create a quick hourly or one day job with pay, time, location and required skills.
  2. View applicants - See matched applicants with profiles, past experience and ratings recommended by skill matching engine.
  3. Select worker - Choose the best-fit worker based on skills, availabolity and reviews.
  4. Confirm booking - Confirm the shift and coordinate final details with the worker through the built in chat.
  5. Rate workers - After the job is done, rate the worker and leave feedback to keep the plattform transparent and reliable.
