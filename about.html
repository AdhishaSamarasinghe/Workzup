Connecting Skills with Opportunities

WorkzUp is an AI-powered short-term job platform that helps Sri Lankan workers and businesses connect quickly, safely and fairly.

Our Story

WorkzUp was built to solve a common problem: short-term job opportunities are scattered, time-consuming to find, and often unreliable.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Empower individuals through fair, fast and secure access to short-term job opportunities.
Vision: Become Sri Lanka’s most trusted short-term job platform — improving livelihoods and helping businesses scale.

Core Values
