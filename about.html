About WorkzUp

WorkzUp is an AI-powered short-term job platform designed to connect Sri Lankan talent with fast, flexible opportunities — safely and efficiently.

Our Story

WorkzUp was created to solve a rising issue in Sri Lanka’s fast-growing gig economy: there was no secure, structured, and modern platform for short-term jobs. Workers had trouble finding reliable opportunities, and employers struggled to find trusted talent. Our team set out to build a platform that bridges this gap through technology, AI, and a user-first approach.

Our Mission

To empower individuals by giving them fair, fast, and secure access to job opportunities while helping businesses connect with the right talent effortlessly.

Our Vision

To become Sri Lanka’s leading short-term job platform — improving lives, supporting businesses, and elevating the future of work for everyone.
